What We Do

Four disciplines, one accountable team

Each engagement is led by a qualified Chartered Accountant, not handed off to a call centre. Here is where we spend most of our time.

01 / AUDIT

Audit & Assurance

Independent, evidence-based audits that satisfy statutory requirements and give management an honest view of the numbers.

  • Statutory audit under Companies Act
  • Tax audit under Section 44AB
  • Internal & management audit
  • Stock & concurrent audit for banks/NBFCs
  • Due diligence for investors & lenders
02 / TAXATION

Taxation

Planning and filing across direct and indirect tax, with representation if a notice ever lands in your inbox.

  • Income tax planning & return filing
  • GST registration, returns & reconciliation
  • TDS/TCS compliance
  • Transfer pricing documentation
  • Assessment & appellate representation
03 / ADVISORY

Financial Services

Numbers that inform decisions, from monthly MIS to the model you take into a fundraise.

  • Bookkeeping & monthly MIS
  • Budgeting & cash-flow forecasting
  • Business valuation
  • Fundraising & project finance support
  • Loan syndication & CMA data preparation
04 / COMPLIANCE

Regulatory Compliance

Every filing your entity owes to the Registrar, on a calendar you never have to remember yourself.

  • Company incorporation & ROC filings
  • Annual returns (AOC-4, MGT-7)
  • LLP & partnership compliance
  • FEMA & RBI filings
  • Payroll, PF & ESIC compliance
How We Work

An engagement, in five stages

Audit and compliance work only holds up if the sequence is followed. This is the order we follow on every engagement.

01

Discovery

We review your books, entity structure and past filings to map where you actually stand.

02

Diagnostic

A gap report flags compliance risk, tax exposure and control weaknesses before they become notices.

03

Planning

We agree a calendar of filings, audits and reviews mapped to your statutory deadlines.

04

Execution

Returns are prepared, reviewed by a partner, and filed with acknowledgements shared with you.

05

Standing Support

We remain on record for queries, assessments and notices for as long as the engagement runs.
